Simplify Your African Expansion with EOR & Payroll

Our flagship solutions for hiring and paying talent across Africa, without the complexity of setting up a local entity.

Employer of Record (EOR)

An EOR allows you to legally hire employees in another country without establishing a local legal entity. We act as the legal employer, handling all HR, payroll, and compliance responsibilities.

  • Speed: Hire talent in days, not months.
  • Compliance: Eliminate risk from unfamiliar labor laws.
  • Cost Control: Avoid high costs of entity setup.

Global Payroll Outsourcing

We manage your entire payroll process with precision, from single-country to complex multi-country consolidation. Our platform can integrate with Xero, QuickBooks, and ADP.
