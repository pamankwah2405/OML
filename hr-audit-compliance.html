When to Conduct an Audit

An independent HR audit is an annual, strategic necessity, ideally every 6–12 months. It should also be triggered by:

  • Major organisational changes (mergers, restructuring)
  • Labour disputes or rising compliance concerns
  • Expansion into new African markets or jurisdictions
  • Implementation of new HR systems or policies
